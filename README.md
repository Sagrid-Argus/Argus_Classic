## What is Argus?

Argus Classic is a custom-built World of Warcraft Vanilla 1.12.1 server designed to answer a simple question:
what if Vanilla could be experienced today, at your own pace, without friction, and without losing what made it great?

This is not a museum piece, and it is not a modern expansion disguised as Vanilla. Argus is a living, evolving version of Vanilla, shaped by deliberate design choices, technical improvements, and a clear goal: deliver a complete, accessible, and deeply engaging experience from the moment you launch the game.

## A Ready-to-Play Vanilla Experience

Argus is built for immediacy. There is no complex setup, no dependency on a populated server, no barriers to entry. You install, you launch, and you play.

Everything has been configured so that the experience works out of the box. Under the hood, the project is powered by a heavily customized CMaNGOS core, continuously updated and refined. Bugs are fixed, systems are adjusted, and new content is integrated regularly. You are not stepping into a static server, but into a version of Vanilla that is actively maintained and improved every month.

<img width="1919" height="1079" alt="Capture d&#39;écran 2026-02-20 231826" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c7caa002-6340-405d-8980-be9bd227cd75" />

## A True “Fresh Server” Experience

Every time Argus is started, the world begins from the same point: level 1, for everyone.

Players and bots alike enter the world simultaneously, recreating the feeling of a true “fresh server”. Early zones are alive, progression is synchronized, and the sense of starting a new journey is preserved in its purest form.

This is a core design principle tied to server initialization. When a server is started, the world begins at level 1 for all players and bots, creating a fresh environment, while the server progression is fully preserved between sessions.

## A Curated Experience by Design

Argus is not meant to be configured or adjusted by the end user. It is intentionally delivered as a fully curated experience.

The server configuration files (.conf) have been carefully tuned to support the project’s design goals: pacing, progression, difficulty, and overall coherence. These settings are not arbitrary. They are part of the experience itself.

For that reason, clear disclaimers are included to discourage modifications. Altering these parameters may break the balance and undermine what Argus is built to deliver.

The intent is simple:
you launch the server, and you experience Argus exactly as it was designed.

## A World That Moves With You

Argus is designed around a fundamental idea: the server lives with the player.

There is no pressure to keep up, no fear of falling behind, no reliance on other players being online. You can stop playing for days, weeks, or months, and when you return, everything is exactly where it should be. Your progression remains intact, your world remains relevant, and your adventure continues naturally.

It feels like a single-player RPG in its flexibility, but it retains the full structure and depth of a massively multiplayer game. You explore, quest, and conquer dungeons in a persistent world that does not move without you.

## Vanilla, Reimagined With Intent

Argus respects the foundation of Vanilla, but it does not hesitate to improve it where it matters.

The early game has been carefully structured for all races, providing a smooth and complete experience up to the first major milestones. Progression is paced to remain faithful, yet more fluid and rewarding for a modern player with limited time.

At level 20, the journey takes a meaningful turn with a fully implemented mount system. Instead of a simple purchase, mounts are earned through dedicated questlines tailored to each race, complete with custom models. This transforms a basic utility into a memorable achievement, while improving mobility at a key moment in progression.

<img width="1553" height="724" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/598c451a-ef20-48a5-9b84-62b9f1a65443" />

## Familiar Content, Reinvented

Argus does not replace Vanilla content. It reworks it from within.

Take the iconic Deadmines. The dungeon remains visually and structurally identical, preserving its identity and nostalgia. But the encounters have been rebalanced, with stronger bosses and a completely revised loot table. The result is a dungeon that demands more from the player and rewards that effort accordingly.

In parallel, new questlines have been introduced to expand the world’s logic. Horde players, for example, are now given a reason to venture into hostile territories early on, adding tension, risk, and a sense of discovery that Vanilla only hinted at.

## Play Solo, Without Playing Alone

Through advanced Playerbots integration, Argus allows you to experience the full group content of Vanilla without relying on other players.

Groups form with proper roles. They travel, fight, adapt, and support you throughout your journey. Dungeons become accessible at any time, not just when a server population allows it. This system does not simplify the game. It makes it consistently playable.

The objective is not to remove the MMO aspect, but to make it available on demand.

## A Project That Evolves With Its Community

Argus is not finished, and that is by design.

It is updated every month with new improvements, systems, and content. Some features are already in place and expanding, such as professions gaining experience, with deeper reworks planned. Others are continuously refined based on testing and real usage.

By playing Argus, you are not just consuming content. You are participating in the evolution of a coherent, long-term vision of Vanilla.

## [ IMPORTANT - SHUTDOWN PROCEDURE ]

Do NOT close windows using the "X".

<img width="427" height="152" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/94d38e6d-9a8a-4483-9048-689293116344" />

Recommended method:

1. In-game chat: type with your character .server shutdown 0 (or: .ser shut 0)
2. Close the client normally
3. Wait for mangosd.exe window to close automatically
4. In realmd.exe window do : Ctrl + C
5. In database window, do : Ctrl + C, Then press: y and Enter.
